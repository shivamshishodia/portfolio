01. About Me
Nice To Meet You!
Hello! My name is Shivam Shishodia, and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2010 when I decided to try editing custom WordPress themes — turns out hacking together a custom reblog button taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!
Fast-forward to today, and I’ve had the privilege of working at a huge corporation and a student-led technical council. Currently, I am working at Dell under MTRC (Make The Right Commitment) pillar, building products for demand and supply space.
I focus on building real-time event-driven architectures. My area of interest also includes Web3 and Amazon Web Services (AWS). In my free time, I love solving coding problems.
